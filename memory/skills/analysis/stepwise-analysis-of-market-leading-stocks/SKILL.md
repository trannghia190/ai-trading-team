# Stepwise Analysis of Market-Leading Stocks

## 1. Assess the macro backdrop
- Summarize the key macro developments: interest rates, FX, foreign capital flows, and supportive policy.
- Identify whether the backdrop is positive, neutral, or negative for the market leaders.

## 2. Identify upside catalysts
- List the standout events or companies: earnings surprises, insider buying, new strategy, IPOs, and similar developments.
- Assess how strongly each catalyst affects the main stock names.

## 3. Analyze the technical picture of the leaders
- Evaluate trend, demand strength, money flow, and breakout or breadth signals on the chart.
- Identify buy zones, support/resistance, and the signals that confirm flow quality.

## 4. Identify the main risks
- Analyze macro weaknesses, sector-specific risk, the potential for flow reversal, or adverse news.
- Set stop-loss thresholds and sensible position sizing for capital management.

## 5. Give a concrete action recommendation
- Propose accumulation zones, sizing, and the flow conditions required for confirmation.
- Warn against chasing and only allocate capital when confirming signals appear.
- Reassess the backdrop and catalysts regularly to keep the recommendation current.
