# Daily Market Summary and Reporting

Comprehensive workflow for synthesizing the Vietnamese stock market session, providing a foundation for both high-level insight and actionable trading strategy.

## 1. Macroeconomic & Market Context
- Record key market indices performance (e.g., VN-Index, VN30) with % change.
- Note aggregate market liquidity (total trading value/volume) and trends versus prior sessions.
- Analyze market breadth (advancers/decliners, A/D ratio) and interpret sentiment (bullish, bearish, neutral).
- Highlight any macro factors (currency movement, monetary policy, global market cues) impacting the session.

## 2. Capital Flow & Sector Dynamics
- Summarize foreign trading activity: net buy/sell values, key stocks with exceptional flows.
- Break down by sector: identify which sectors/groups outperformed or lagged, noting any unusual rotation or leadership shifts.
- Highlight notable company- or sector-specific news that influenced price action.

## 3. Technical Evaluation
- Assess technical signals: index trend (short/medium-term), support/resistance zones, signs of distribution/accumulation.
- Review basis (e.g., VN30F1M vs spot), derivative market cues, and implications for short-term trading.

## 4. Opportunities & Risks Assessment
- Explicitly list near-term market opportunities: attractive sectors, potential technical rebounds, themes, or stocks showing strength.
- Identify primary risks: breadth deterioration, liquidity withdrawal, overbought/oversold signals, macro headwinds, or sector vulnerabilities.
- Rate market phase (e.g., uptrend, correction, sideway, bear phase).

## 5. Strategic Recommendations
- Recommend NAV allocation (cash vs equity) with specific % target based on the current risk/reward landscape.
- Define conditional criteria for opening new positions (e.g., upon confirmation of support, sector rotation, or flow reversal).
- Suggest hedging or stop-loss triggers where relevant.

## 6. Risk Management
- Outline clear risk management rules for the recommended strategy: max drawdown, per-trade risk, cash reserves, reaction to unexpected market shocks.
- Offer scenario-based action plans (e.g., if index breaks down/up through key levels).

## 7. Reporting Template
- Present all above in a concise, professional summary with bullet points, metrics, and actionable conclusions for decision-makers.

## 8. Optional: Deep-Dive Modules
- Attach sector- or ticker-specific mini-reports, technical deep-dives, or thematic analyses as appendices when warranted by market context.
