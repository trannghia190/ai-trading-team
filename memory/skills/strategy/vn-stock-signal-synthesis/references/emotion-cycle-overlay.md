# Emotion Cycle Overlay

## Purpose
Use this as an overlay after price -> event -> flow analysis; do not use it to override disciplined setup rules.

## Emotion states
- Cold
- Stable
- Warming
- Crowded
- Euphoric

## Interpreting states
### Cold
- low turnover, compressed volume, few headlines
- it can mean accumulation, but it can also mean there is no compelling story
- wait for a chart turn or a new catalyst before buying

### Stable
- trading is normal and not at an emotional extreme
- rely primarily on the original setup read

### Warming
- volume, turnover, and sector participation are rising gradually
- suitable for monitoring breakouts or quality pullbacks

### Crowded
- the narrative is hot and many people are looking at the same trade
- if the chart is already stretched, switch to no-chase or hold-no-add framing

### Euphoric
- turnover, volume, and headline heat are surging
- sell-the-news risk is high and incremental reward compresses quickly
- this state is usually better suited to reduce, hold-no-add, or watch-only than buy-now

## Guardrails
- Good thesis + cold != auto buy
- Good thesis + euphoric + stretched chart != buy now
- Weak thesis + cold != an automatic contrarian edge

## Suggested output phrase
- Trade state: early/actionable, confirmed but extended, quality thesis poor timing, vulnerable to sell-the-news, low-edge/skip
