# Setup Library

## Purpose
Standardize the main setup types for VN stock swing trades using a stock-mcp-first approach.

## Core setup taxonomy
- Accumulation pullback
- Shrink-volume pullback
- Base breakout
- Box support buy
- Box breakout conversion
- Bottom-volume reversal
- Catalyst-day markup
- Extended trend / no-chase
- Distribution risk
- Breakdown

## Macro Overlays (New)
- **Fear/Greed Check**: Avoid chasing breakouts during Extreme Greed.
- **Liquidity Check**: Reduce size if interbank overnight rates spike sharply, signaling tight liquidity.

## 1. Accumulation pullback
Context:
- the trend is still intact and price is pulling back to a reasonable support area
Trigger:
- support holds and price reaction is constructive
Invalidation:
- support breaks on poor volume behavior
Avoid:
- the pullback occurs right before a major event risk without enough buffer

## 2. Shrink-volume pullback
Context:
- the stock is in healthy markup or accumulation
- volume contracts during the pullback
Trigger:
- the close holds the MA or support and the rebound candle is constructive
Invalidation:
- volume expands aggressively on the downside and a key MA is lost
Avoid:
- the pullback turns into prolonged distribution

## 3. Base breakout
Context:
- there is a clear accumulation base and a clear resistance line
Trigger:
- close above resistance with expanding volume
Invalidation:
- the breakout fails and price falls back below resistance
Avoid:
- chasing after price is already too far above support after 1-2 sessions

## 4. Box support buy
Context:
- the sideways range is clear and breakout has not happened yet
Trigger:
- price is near the bottom of the box and support reaction is constructive
Invalidation:
- the bottom of the box breaks
Avoid:
- buying in the middle of the box or near the top of it

## 5. Box breakout conversion
Context:
- the stock has just broken out of the box cleanly
Trigger:
- breakout close plus follow-through, or a pullback that holds the box edge
Invalidation:
- price quickly falls back into the box
Avoid:
- the breakout lacks volume or breadth/flow is fighting against it

## 6. Bottom-volume reversal
Context:
- after a deep decline, volume surges and price starts stabilizing
Trigger:
- follow-through appears or the low holds successfully
Invalidation:
- the recent low is lost again
Avoid:
- bottom-fishing just because of one strong green session without catalyst or flow support

## 7. Catalyst-day markup
Context:
- a strong event has just been confirmed
Trigger:
- use the same logic as the catalyst-execution framework
Invalidation:
- sell-the-news / fail follow-through
Avoid:
- using LO as a psychological compromise during a runaway move

## 8. Extended trend / no-chase
Context:
- the thesis is still good, but price is already too far from a clean entry
Action:
- hold/no-add or watch-only
Avoid:
- trying to rationalize BUY NOW when reward/risk is badly skewed

## Supporting indicators only
- MA cross: only a supporting signal
- RSI/MACD: useful for timing, but should not override bad structure
- micro-patterns: only meaningful when they sit inside a larger setup
