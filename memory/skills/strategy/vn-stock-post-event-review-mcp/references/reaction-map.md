# Post-Event Reaction Map

## Reaction matrix

### Good event + good reaction
- Follow-through confirms the move
- HOLD or ADD ON CONFIRMATION is reasonable if the stock is not extended yet

### Good event + weak reaction
- This can easily be sell-the-news behavior or a sign of heavy supply
- Prefer HOLD / NO ADD, or even REDUCE if distribution is clear

### Neutral event + strong reaction
- The market may be pricing a bigger story than the headline itself
- It is only actionable if the chart and flow agree

### Bad event + resilient reaction
- The bad news may already have been priced in
- Do not turn automatically bearish, but wait for another confirming session

### Bad event + bad reaction
- The thesis or timing is broken
- Prefer SELL / EXIT or WATCH FOR RE-ENTRY
